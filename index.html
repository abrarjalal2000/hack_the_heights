John Zulewski is a sophomore at Boston College from Buffalo, NY. He is interested in the intersection of technology and social impact. Currently, he is working with tech startup GiveCard to understand the legal issues with giving debit cards to the homeless, as well as assisting them with building better relationships with their stakeholders. He previously worked with Allston-Brighton Community Development Center and was a founding member of Fit Nation BC, a nonprofit focused on afterschool physical activity.
